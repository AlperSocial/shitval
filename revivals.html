Roblox Revival List

  • Serene

    Serene is a 2014-2021 revival that is in development. This revival was known as "RoConomy" until it's rebrand. It is rumored to be available for Windows, Linux (with Wine) and Android in the future.

  • GooberBlox

    GooberBlox is a 2016E Roblox revival with an open-source launcher that was made by mathmark824.

    Pros:

    • Finished client
    • Actively documented development
    • Large player base
    • Custom webpage

    Cons:

  • Virtubrick

    Virtubrick is an upcoming 2016 roblox revival that is actively work in progress.

    Pros:

    • Well documented development
    • Custom webpage

    Cons:

  • idk16

    IDK16 is an old Roblox revival, trying to revive the era of late 2016. idk16 is trying to use original Roblox software for playing from this period of time, ensuring there is the best accuracy with the experience. idk16 currently support Windows, Android, Linux with Wine and iOS.

    Pros:

    • Supports android, iOS, linux (wine)
    • Owner does testing events sometimes

    Cons:

    • In testing
    • Community isn't that active

  • Atlas (aka Project: Reblock)

    Atlas seems to be an upcoming 2015 Roblox Revival. We currently do not know anything about this revival as it is being worked on.

  • Stratus

    Stratus is a 2016 Economy Simulator revival that was made for users who missed ECS. This revival is currently playable on PC.

    Pros:

    • Active Community
    • Economy Focused

    Cons:

    • ECS Revival (This type of revival is concidered not good for some people)

  • Kiseki is an upcoming open-source 2016 project that is made for users to host and have fun. Kiseki is currently in a quality assurance stage and support Windows and Linux.

    Pros:

    • Active Development
    • Very unique
    • Many amazing features that hasn't been seen before

    Cons:

    • The discord is only for updates.

  • Silkblox is an anonymous revival made for those who are on the dark side of the community! This revival is unfortunately still in development.

    Pros:

    • Unique
    • Somewhat active community

    Cons:

    • No updates on development
