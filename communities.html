Roblox Communities

  • Shitval

    The official Shitval Discord server.

    Pros:

    • Somewhat active
    • Great place to connect with users

    Cons:

    • Other languages are not allowed

  • ORC Central

    ORC Central is a discord server for news, chatting and etc. ORC Central was formerly known as "ORC News"

    Pros:

    • Somewhat active
    • Diverse

    Cons:

    • Small community
    • Unprofessional looking Server

  • Rchive

    Rchive is a community that is dedicated to preserving old Roblox media. This is a great recourse for old Roblox.

    Pros:

    • Great recourse/source
    • Diverse

    Cons:

    • I haven't seen any cons of this community, let me know if there are any.

  • Kattus Community

    The Kattus Community is a discord server that was made by the YouTuber Kattus for his fans.

    Pros:

    • Silly channels
    • Frequent events

    Cons:

    • The channels are just a mess

  • ORC2 Reddit

    The ORC2 Reddit is an replacement for the current ORC Reddit made by qzip with much better rules.

  • ORC Reddit

    The ORC Reddit is probably one of the most popular discussion forums in the community right now with a total of 3k+ members.

    Pros:

    • Great source for finding revivals

    Cons:

    • Somewhat toxic community
    • Dead
